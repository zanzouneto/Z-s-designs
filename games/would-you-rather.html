Drinking Game Option

In the non-drinking game, each player gets a unique question.
In the drinking game, the same question is posed to all players.


  1. players answer the question simultaneously.
    • Use cards to identify your answer: green for the first option, red for the second.
    • Alternatively, use your hands: one finger for the first option, two fingers for the second.
    • Or get cretive and find your own identifiers.
  2. Count the votes for each option.
  3. Minority voters drink.
  4. You can abstain from answering a question by taking a double drink.
